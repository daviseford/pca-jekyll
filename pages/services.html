We offer the following services, and much, much more.

Classic Cars

Park Center Automotive owner David Ford has taken home dozens of trophies from automotive shows all over the country. We're happy to offer classic and antique vehicle restoration - experienced, show-winning mechanics diligently improving your pride and joy.

Restoration

Did you just snag a dusty barn find and you want to bring it back to its original glory? Park Center Automotive specializes in carefully restoring, improving, and tuning your classic treasure. We can help fix rotting upholstery, rusted body work, and stopped engines.

Show Preparation

Your car is shiny, beautiful, and stunning - but it's still not taking home the first place trophy at your local car show. Come to us and let us help you with the little details that make or break a car on the competitive circuit.

Oil Changes

22% of vehicles have low or dirty engine oil. Proactively changing a vehicles’ oil and filter will help the engine continue to work at its best and prevent a costly repair down the road.

Brake Repair

If your brakes are squealing, you’ve come to the right place. We'll make sure that your brakes are serviced, including all replacement parts like pads, drums, and rotors.

Heat & A/C

A properly working climate conditioning system keeps you and everyone else in the vehicle comfortable all the way to your destination.

Check Engine

On many vehicles the Check Engine light is triggered by a wide range of causes. We can diagnose, estimate, and repair any issues affecting your vehicle's performance.

Tires/Wheels

Whether you just need a little air or a nail extracted from a tire, Park Center Automotive can help. We can help you pick out the perfect wheels and tires for all seasons and reasons.

Transmission

Your transmission is the absolute heart of your vehicle. Treat it right by replacing your transmission fluid to help preserve the life of the transmission.
